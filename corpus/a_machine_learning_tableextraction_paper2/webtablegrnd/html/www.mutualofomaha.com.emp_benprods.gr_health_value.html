Value Added Features

PPO & HMO/POS
PPO Value Added Features
24-hour customer service
We offer a 24-hour toll-free customer service line.
Negotiate Best Price We negotiate the best available price on designated claims with providers. In so doing, when insureds find it necessary to access medical care outside our network, we consistently gain significant savings for the client and employees. Health Experience Reporting Our Health Experience Reporting provides feedback on how employees are using their health plan. It's a useful tool for pinpointing where benefit dollars are spent and for fine-tuning the plan. Policyholders can have Advanced Network Specific Reference System (ANSRS) installed for on-site access to our reporting capabilities. Current Trends Current Trends is our annual publication that tracks health care utilization and cost trends. Derived from our Personal Claims Administration data, it is available online. This helpful information may be used to compare our experience to overall trends.
HMO/POS Value Added Features

To help ensure Quality Improvement, we measure health care indicators every year. Annual goals are established for many preventive care provisions such as children receiving immunizations.

Periodic random member satisfaction surveys help ensure our plans are meeting the needs of members.

Disease Management
Healthy directions sm * , our disease management program consists of prevention efforts and education of participants with asthma, diabetes or congestive heart failure (CHF).

Healthy directions

seeks to:
  • Improve medical outcomes.
  • Optimize the overall use of health care resources.
  • Reduce health care expenses.

All of our commercial group insured medical business - - HMO, POS, PPO and Indemnity - - automatically benefit from this program with no change in premium rates

. For ASO business, the cost of the program is built into the administration fee.

Participation is voluntary.

Healthy directions

does not replace the care received from the participant's physician -- the participant and his/her physician remain in charge of the participant’s treatment plan.

Im mediate Access to Current Provider Directories
We provide immediate access to current Provider Directories via Directories-On-Demand. Directories-On-Demand are medical and dental provider directories that you create -- at your desk! Your clients and their employees may do so, too. Providers may be located online by name, specialty and location. Non-participating providers may be nominated online for inclusion in our network, too.

You may select the provider directory that meets your needs:
Complete directory Covers a specific geographic area, similar to our traditional paper directories, but because the data is updated monthly, there are no addenda to worry about.
City/state directory Create a directory by the city you choose. It can include all, or specific, provider types and specialties.
Address-based directory Specific to geographic parameters you select. This directory can also include all, or specific, provider types and specialties.

There's even an option to include the client's name on the directory cover. A PDF file of your directory will be created offline, so your computer won't be tied up. And, you may immediately request additional Mutual of Omaha directories. When your request is completed, you'll receive an e-mail with a link to your directory. Click on that link and you're there! And, the PDF file can be sent as an attachment to an e-mail, or it can be posted to customers' intranet sites.

We research our networks for a full range of providers in the prospective client's locations, using GeoNetworks, a product of GeoAccess. We calculate precise distances from providers to the prospective client's locations, and generate maps plotting member and provider locations. We also analyze our existing provider networks for possible improvements.
