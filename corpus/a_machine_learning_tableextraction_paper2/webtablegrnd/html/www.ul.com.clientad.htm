Customer service

Our client advisors can give you a helping hand

UL Client Advisors are located at each UL laboratory facility in the United States. They answer questions, provide information and assist clients as they work with UL on testing and certification projects. Although they are based in the U.S., our advisors assist clients in all regions of the world.

They are an excellent resource for helping customers understand the product submittal process, identifying the UL Technical experts in their industry, and finding out about UL's adjunct services.

Fill out and submit the client advisor contact form below and someone who handles your region will get back to you as soon as possible. Or contact them by phone, fax or e-mail .

Check out our Global Resources pages for specific information on UL activities in your region.

If you are interested in employment opportunities with UL please visit our career information section.
