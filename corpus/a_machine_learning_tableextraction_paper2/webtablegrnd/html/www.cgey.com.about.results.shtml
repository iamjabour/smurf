1999 Results

The Directoire of Cap Gemini S.A., at its meeting in Paris on March 15, 2000, chaired by Serge Kampf, agreed the consolidated accounts of Cap Gemini Ernst & Young for the year ended December 31, 1999.

Confirming the provisional results published on January 26, these consolidated and audited accounts show the following :

  • revenue of 4.310 billion euros, an increase of 9 % over the same figure for 1998 (7 % at constant rates and structure);
  • operating profit of 469 million euros, representing 10.9 % of revenue, as opposed to 10.3 % last year;
  • net income, exclusive of minority interests and before amortization of goodwill , of 294 million euros, versus 218 million euros in 1998;
  • net profit, exclusive of minority interests , of 266 million euros, compared to 188 million euros last year, which represents 6.2 % of revenue (4.8 % in 1998);
  • consolidated net Group equity of 2,638 million euros, compared to fixed assets of 1,950 million euros.

1999 First-half results

The Directoire of Cap Gemini SA approved the consolidated and audited accounts of Cap Gemini Ernst & Young for the first-half 1999, which confirm the provisional results published on July 6 and show the following (*):

  • revenue of FF 14,444 M, up 16.2 % at constant structure and exchange rates ;
  • operating income of FF 1,469 M, up 34.1 % as opposed to the first-half of 1998 (FF 1,097 M) and 10.2 % of revenue as opposed to 8.8 % last year ;
  • net income exclusive of minority interests of FF 878 M (including an exceptional income of FF 150 M) compared to last year?s figure of FF 454 M (which included an exceptional income of FF 18 M), that is an increase, setting aside the exceptional income, of 67 % ;
  • EARNINGS PER SHARE come to FF 13 compared with FF 7 for first-half 1998.
