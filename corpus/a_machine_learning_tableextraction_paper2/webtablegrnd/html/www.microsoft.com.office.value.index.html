Why Office 2000



Business Value

Office 2000 can help your organization succeed in today's business environment because it finally makes an intranet a practical collaboration tool. New features enable your employees to use the productivity tools they're already familiar with to work together more effectively over your existing intranet infrastructure.

Read our Business Value Overview for the "big picture" of the main benefits of moving to Office 2000:

  • Enhanced communication and collaboration
  • More targeted data access and analysis
  • Better management and improved ease of use
