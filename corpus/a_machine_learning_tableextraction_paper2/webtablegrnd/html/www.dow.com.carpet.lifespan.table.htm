LIFESPAN backings will be TERRA-COTTA + in color with the brand name printed on the outer surface. The woven polypropylene fabric shall be a minimum of 11 pic for LIFESPAN MG.

LIFESPAN polyurethane carpet backing is readily identifiable by the unique TERRA-COTTA + color of the composite backing and the trademark LIFESPAN seen prominently on the woven fabric undersurface.

+ The color TERRA-COTTA is a trademark of The Dow Chemical Company

NOTE: Some face constructions and backing classifications may be inappropriate when roller mobility is a concern; therefore, Dow recommends that you first consult with the carpet manufacturer to choose the correct face construction and product class for your particular application.

LIFESPAN carpet backing is available with an antimicrobial option. Consult with your carpet manufacturer regarding NBS smoke chamber and flooring radiant panel ratings for specific carpet styles with LIFESPAN carpet backing. If an "or equal" product is considered, we suggest that smoke and flammability data be developed on the "or equal" product as a composite of the carpet and underlayment as they will be installed.
