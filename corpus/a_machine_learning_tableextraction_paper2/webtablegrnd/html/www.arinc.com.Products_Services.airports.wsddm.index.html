• Weather Support to Deicing Decision Making
WSDDM is a weather forecasting tool that provides real-time snow gauge data and predictive snow and ice intensities used for managing snow removal and aircraft deicing.

WSDDM information is provided in a single, easy-to-read display that shows current and two-hour historical weather information, as well as a 30-minute “nowcast” of snow intensities.

The system requires minimal training to operate and no special meteorological knowledge to interpret. Users get a maximum amount of weather information at a glance:

  • Doppler weather radar

  • Real-time snow gauge data accurately measuring the amount of liquid water content in the snow

  • Meteorological data updated every 1 to 5 minutes

  • Two-hour timeline graph of snow and ice intensities at specific locations within the terminal area

  • 30-minute nowcasts of snowfall

    The WSDDM 30-minute accurate forecast is a proven tool that helps the air transport industry reduce winter operational costs by reducing flight delays, diversions, and re-deicings; maximizing equipment, deicing fluids, and other resources; and improving the management of holdover times.
