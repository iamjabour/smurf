We offer the standard certificate four times during the academic year and the advanced certificate two times. We strongly recommend that you take required courses for either certificate in the sequence listed. We also recommend that you take one "track" from beginning to end.



For dates, locations, and descriptions of all courses, click on the links in the tables below.



If you have any questions about either certificate or any of the courses, please email fritzschev@pdx.edu or call 503-725-5846.



The Winter/Spring program for the standard certificate at the CAPITAL Center in Beaverton runs from January to May, 2001.

We have scheduled the first three of the four required courses. We will provide dates for the final course, as well as all other spring 2001 courses, soon.
