The College Level Examination Program is a method for receiving college credit for what you already may have learned through experience, study on your own, etc. CLEP exam schedules will be determined annually by the Coordinator of Admissions Testing. For the current schedule, click here or contact 1-800-OLEMISS (in Mississippi) or (662) 915-7226.

The times and locations of the test will be mailed to the student after receipt of the application approximately two weeks prior to the test date. Each test will take approximately two hours.

You must submit a CLEP application ONE MONTH prior to the date of the test administration. Applications are to be submitted to the Admissions Office (145 Martindale) between 8:00 A.M. and 5:00 P.M. Monday through Friday with the $7.00 administrative fee (cash or check/money order payable to The University of Mississippi Testing Center). The CLEP exam fee ($46 per test) is paid on the scheduled test date.

PLEASE NOTE: Credit granted by The University of Mississippi for CLEP subject examinations is given according to the ACE (American Council on Education) recommended scores. Use in a University degree program is subject to approval by the student's Academic Dean and Department Chair.

Scores and Credit

Scores are generally received from CLEP within one month. Scores will be sent to the student's address and to the Admissions Office of the school to which the student requested his/her scores be sent.

Scores sent to The University of Mississippi will be recorded on the student's transcript after 12 hours of resident credit have been completed. The total number of hours one may earn in any of the credit-by-examination programs is 63.
