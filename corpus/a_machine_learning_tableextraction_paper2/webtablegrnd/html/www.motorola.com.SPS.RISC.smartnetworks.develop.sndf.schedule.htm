Mark your calendar: May 20-23, 2001.



Download Conference Schedule (PDF format)



Sunday, May 20:

10:00am - 8:00pm Arrivals and registration
1:00pm - 5:00pm Smart Networks Sunday Seminars
6:00pm - 9:00pm Attendee Welcome reception

Monday, May 21:

8:00am - 9:45am Opening general session featuring Chuck Martin, author of "Net Future" and "Max-e Marketing in the Net Future," and Daniel Artusi, Corporate Vice President and General Manager, Motorola Networking and Computing Systems Group
10:00am - 5:45pm Forum breakout sessions
12:00pm - 8:30pm Smart Networks Technology Lab open
6:30pm - 8:30pm Evening reception in Technology Lab

Tuesday, May 22:

8:00am - 8:50pm General session featuring Dr. Larry Walker, CEO & Co-Founder, C-Port, a Motorola Company
9:00am - 5:45pm Forum breakout sessions
9:00am - 6:00pm Smart Networks Technology Lab open
6:30pm - 10:00pm Evening party at House of Blues

Wednesday, May 23:

8:00am - 9:00am General session featuring a panel discussion with leading Motorola networking and computing systems architects
9:15am - 11:30am Forum breakout sessions
11:30am - 12:30pm Closing general session featuring panel discussion with Motorola Executives and General Managers
12:30am - 1:30pm Survey Handling and Parting Gift distribution from Motorola
1:30pm Afternoon departures

Please note: Forum schedule subject to change.

At the conclusion of the closing general session on Wednesday, May 23, 2001, attendees are encouraged to complete the Post-Event Survey to receive a set of Motorola TalkAbout® radios - compliments of the Smart Networks Alliance and Motorola. (US$180 value)

Much more than a new way for groups of people to keep in touch over short distances, TalkAbout® radios are built to Motorola's uncompromising standards of quality. Small and light, they easily fit in a pocket, purse, fanny pack or backpack. They're even fun to look at. You can make TalkAbout® radios part of your neighborhood, outdoor and leisure activities for free by simply providing us with valuable feedback on your Smart Networks Developer Forum experience.
