Introduction

The Third International Radiocarbon Inter-comparison (TIRI) was the last such exercise conducted. A new round (FIRI) is now underway.

Plots with laboratory comments

These plots give a graphical comparison of the Oxford results to the mean, median and inter-quartile range of all participating labs and specific points for the other AMS labs. For comparison, they also show the results of high precision measurements made at Belfast.

The results are generally in good agreement with the means and medians. They are always in very good agreement with the results from Belfast.
