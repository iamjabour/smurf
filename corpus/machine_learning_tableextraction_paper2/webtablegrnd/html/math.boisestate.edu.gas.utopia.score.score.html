The Utopia vocal and piano score was donated to the archive by Larry Byler . This is a complete vocal score, consisting of all of the songs as Postscript and Adobe Acrobat PDF files, and the dialogue as Microsoft Word files. Any comments or questions on them should be directed to Larry.

Adobe Acrobat Portable Document Format (PDF) Files

You do NOT need a PostScript printer to work with these files. They can be both viewed and printed on any microcomputer equipped with Adobe Acrobat Reader software.

The PDF files include the music scores only, and not the spoken dialogue. The spoken dialogue is provided separately as Microsoft Word 95 .doc files in a self-extracting zip file .

The PDF files are provided as both combined files for each act, and as individual files for each song.
