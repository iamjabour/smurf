WELCOME TO THE SCORE 2000 SCORE II Official Website. The Year 2000 SCORE II Leadership Program, (SCORE II-2K), was held from June 30 to July 6, 2000 at The CNIB and IBM Canadian National Offices in Toronto, Ontario. This year's program consisted of a leadership and team building component, recreation component and a computer component focused on Website Design.
