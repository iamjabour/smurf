The Value of Renewables

Weather-related disasters are becoming commonplace in our society, and their costs are overwhelming citizens and the insurance industry alike. In 1998 alone, the insurance industry lost more than $10 billion to such catastrophes. Learn more .

But renewable energy and efficiency technologies can help in many ways. Buildings incorporating energy efficiency technologies are better at withstanding severe weather conditions, and renewable energy systems are designed to take even the most brutal weather conditions. Furthermore, all of these technologies can help during recovery, both by providing electricity when the electricity grid is down over a wide area and by requiring less of that electricity during this crucial time.
