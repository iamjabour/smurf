This Day (Lagos)

April 4, 2001

> Posted to the web April 4, 2001

Uche Obike

> Lagos

The newly appointed United State's Ambassador to Nigeria, His Excellency, Mr. Howard Jetter will today lead a six-man delegation to The Nigerian Stock Exchange's Corporate Headquarters in Lagos.

The visit is to enable Jetter and his team have first hand knowledge of operation of The Nigerian Stock Exchange, especially, its Automated Trading System (ATS) and Central Securities Cleanng System (CSCS). The team will hold talks with the management of The Nigerian Stock Exchange on investment opportunities that abound on the market. The diplomatic team will also watch the trading session and exchange ideas with the stockbrokers.

With the internationalization of The Nigerian Stock Exchange and automation of its operation, foreign portfolio investors are on the queue for investment opportunities through the market.
