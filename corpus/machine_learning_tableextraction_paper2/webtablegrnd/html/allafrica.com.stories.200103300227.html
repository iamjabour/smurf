The Post (Lusaka)

March 30, 2001

> Posted to the web March 30, 2001

Kennedy Gondwe

> Lusaka

The Midlands table tennis open tournament has been set for this Saturday at the Bank of Zambia Sports complex in Lusaka.

Midlands Table Tennis Association (MTTA) chairman Harrison Kalumba said the tournament would start at 09:00 hours. "The participation fee per player is pegged at K5,000.

All participating clubs should come with at least 10 players," he said. He said people from the communities and companies were also free to participate.

According to a programme of events, the tournament is expected to draw participants from under-14 boys and girls to men's singles. Doubles involving under-20 boys and those above the age group will also be played.
