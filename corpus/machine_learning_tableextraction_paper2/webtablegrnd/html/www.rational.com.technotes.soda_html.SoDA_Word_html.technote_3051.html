QUESTION: 
My numbering in SoDA for Word generated documents is not correct, 
what can I do to fix this?
ANSWER: 
Figure numbers and outline numbers may be numbered incorrectly  in 
generated documents.
 
There are two ways to solve this problem:
	Use GenerateReport instead of GenerateDocument
	Run the macro:  Tools->Macro->Macros->mRemoveHiddenText
 
This macro removes all hidden text and should fix the figure numbering.
 
For more information, contact Rational Software Technical Support.
