Shock Mounts



TSM4000

Table Shock Mount

The TSM4000 Table Shock Mount is specially designed for fixed microphone installations in tables or podiums in broadcast studios or meeting rooms and provides perfect shock absorbtion for the installed microphone. TSM4000 is recommended for usage with special short versions of the Compact Active Poles from the Flamingo Series and the Compact Microphone types with Lemo-connectors. It is mounted from under the table plate, where a hole needs to be drilled to allow the goose-neck and the microphone to be installed. The rough edges of the drilled hole in the table plate is covered with the enclosed plastic cover for a 25mm hole.
