2001 STATE SURPLUS PROPERTY AUCTION SCHEDULE

The following is the State of Minnesota's 2001 Surplus Property Auction schedule.

We encourage you to take advantage of our Auction Hot Line (651-296-1056). The Hot Line is updated about 10 days before each auction with general information about what will be available, when, and where.

All auction dates are Saturdays (except the bison auction which is held on a Wednesday). Inspection begins at 8 a.m.; the sale begins at 9:30 a.m. All sales are "AS-IS, WHERE-IS" to the highest bidder. We make no warranties, expressed or implied, nor any claims as to the condition or operability of the items, so please inspect all items carefully.

Minnesota sales tax will be charged on all items, except vehicles, which are taxed at the time the title is transferred.

Cash, certified checks, traveler's checks, money orders, or personal checks will be accepted on the day of the sale.

Please DO NOT bring children under the age of 13 or pets to the State Auctions.

NO CREDIT CARDS WILL BE ACCEPTED!!
