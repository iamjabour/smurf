Value of The Federal Minimum Wage, 1938-1997

The chart and table below are a representation of the minimum wage over the last 43 years in 1997 dollars adjusted for inflation using the CPI-U . The shaded blocks on the chart below represent years in which the Fair Labor Standards Act was amended to raise the minimum wage.

The table below shows the actual minimum wage (Nominal Dollars) and the relative value of the minimum wage (1998 Dollars). The bolded rows represent years in which the Fair Labor Standards Act was amended to raise the minimum wage. This table is also available in PDF format.
