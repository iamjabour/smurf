The North Coast Stock Index

The North Coast Stock Index (NCSI) is a performance indicator of local stocks developed by The Press Democrat and Stephen Lewis, director of the SSU Center for Regional Economics. It tracks the weekly change in the value of a portfolio comprised of a single share of stock in 20 companies on the North Coast.

A complete list of NCSI companies is available.
