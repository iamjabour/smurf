FAQs regarding strategic planning - geared primarily toward non-profit organizations but with enough detail and general information to provide useful information to any strategic planner.
Strategic Planning Handbook - http://www.state.az.us/ospb/planning.html#Handbook

Managing for Results is the strategic planning handbook for Arizonas state agencies. Although this is a state government manual, it has a lot of useful information on the mechanics of the planning process (planning components, SWOT assessments, vision/mission, performance measurement) that is applicable to any planning effort. All documents available in pdf and html formats.

Links to state governments having guidelines or handbooks designed to assist their agencies with strategic planning or business planning. Although directed toward state governmental planning, much of the information can also be applied to other levels of government, non-profits and the private sector.
Director's Handbook of Questions - http://www.directorshandbook.com

Over 2,500 good questions for directors to ask to keep CEOs on their toes! A "thinking tool" to prepare CEOs, directors, and staff for board meetings, strategic planning meetings, shareholder meetings.

Strategic Planning Template for the state of Texas. Not so much a "template" but rather a nice set of graphics that depict the overall planning process.
