Definitions:

An Intensive is an all-day workshop in one of eight or nine categories. Submit 3 poems or 10 pages of screenplay, play, non-fiction work, short story or novel to be worshipped by the instructor in class. Select only one or the Thursday field trip.

A One-on-One is a 30-minute private consultation with the presenter(s) of your choice. Submit a sample of your work in advance: 8 pgs. in short fiction, 5 pgs. of poetry, a short outline and 10 pgs. of a novel, screenplay or play. You may sign up for one or more of these on a first come, first serve basis. Cost: $50 for 30 min.

Lecture Sessions : Presenters may give inspirational and instructive lectures or discuss nuts and bolts.

Editor/Agent/Publisher Chat Rooms , an opportunity to ask the questions you want answered in a small informal setting.

Logistically, we cannot tape these sessions, however you may record sessions if you wish. Bring your own tape recorder.
