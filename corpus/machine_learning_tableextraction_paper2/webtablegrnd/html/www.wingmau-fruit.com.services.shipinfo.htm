Sailing Schedule

We are currently having business connections with the following shipping companies: HMM, MAERSK, OOCL and APL . With long-term friendly co-operation with them, we are in a position to provide the fastest shipping service with flexibility and convenience. We are able to supervise and control the overall transportation more adequately, and responsive to whatever problems might occur.
We offer our customers with efficient, economical and professional shipping services with guaranteed satisfaction.
