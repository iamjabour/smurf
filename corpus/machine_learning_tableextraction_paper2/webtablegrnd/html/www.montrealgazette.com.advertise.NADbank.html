1999 NADbank® Study Results

Version française

MontrealGazette.com generates more than 3.9 million page impressions and attracts an average of 350,000 visitors each month, ranking it among the most popular Montreal-based sites. NADbank ® — the principal research arm of the Canadian newspaper industry — revealed in its 1999 study that 251,700 of these visitors are from Montreal. In addition, the NADbank ® results show that 95,700 of the Montrealers who visit the site are not Gazette readers.
