Commission Schedule
Introduction

A separate commission is charged for each security bought or sold through Vanguard Brokerage Services®. This commission schedule is subject to change.



Stocks

Transaction Method Commission*
Access VBS® (online trading) $20 or $0.02 per share, whichever is greater
AutoBroker® (automated telephone trading) $40 or $0.02 per share, whichever is greater
Orders Desk (through a VBS Associate) $45 + $0.03 per share


*For stocks priced less than $1, a commission of $30 + 3% of principal will apply. For foreign securities, a $75 processing fee is added to the commission rate listed above.



Options

Transaction Method Commission**
Access VBS (online trading) $30 + $1.50 per contract
AutoBroker (automated telephone trading) $30 + $2 per contract
Orders Desk (through a VBS Associate) $30 + $2.50 per contract


**For option exercises and assignments, the rate is $45 + $0.03 per share. A separate commission is charged for opening, closing, or exercising an option position.



FundAccess® (Non-Vanguard Mutual Funds)

Commission
No-Transaction-Fee Funds (NTF) No Fee
  • The minimum initial investment is $5,000 for nonretirement accounts and $1,000 for IRAs. The minimum subsequent purchase is $1,000 for both account types.
  • A redemption fee of 1% or $50, whichever is greater, will apply for NTF shares held less than one year. This fee is waived for shares transferred to VBS from another institution.
  • There is no fee for dollar-cost averaging.
Transaction-Fee Funds (TF) $35 for any order size
  • VBS normally defaults to the initial and subsequent investment minimums for the specific fund, as listed in the fund's prospectus.
  • A fee of $3 per scheduled transaction is charged for dollar-cost averaging.
