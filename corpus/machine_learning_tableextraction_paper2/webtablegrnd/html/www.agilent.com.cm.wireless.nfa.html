Noise Figure Products

Whether you are working with systems, subsystems, subassemblies, or discrete devices, each of your components add noise that affects the overall performance of your receiver. By measuring and reducing noise figure, you can give your components a competitive edge.

Agilent Technologies provide a range of noise figure measurements solutions that allow you to make accurate, fast and repeatable noise figure measurements on the production line as well as in the lab.

Easy measurement of amplifiers and frequency converting devices

• Flexible and intuitive user interface

• Color graphical display with markers and limit lines

• Automatic control of LO via second GPIB



Frequency range to 26.5 GHz and beyond ….

• One-box analyzers to 26.5 GHz

• Custom solutions to even higher frequencies



Accurate and repeatable results

• Low instrument uncertainty

• Low instrument emissions

• Frequency dependent loss compensation



ALSO NEW SNS Series Noise Sources

• Automatic upload of ENR data to the NFA speeds overall setup time and eliminates manual entry errors

• Automatic self-temperature monitoring for improved accuracy
