The SBA/SCORE Business Information Center (BIC)

The SBA/SCORE Business Information Center (BIC) is located in downtown Kansas City, Missouri and is open from 8:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday and from 8:30 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. on Thursday. Metered curb parking is available. If you are not sure of our location, see the MAP .

The BIC is closed on all government-recognized holidays.

Top of Page

BIC Resources (Library, computers, counseling)

The BIC has an extensive small business reference library of hard copy books and publications and current management video tapes to help entrepreneurs plan their business, expand an existing business, or venture into new business areas. Computer resources, hardware and software, are available for client use. SCORE counselors are on-site (from 9:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday and from 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. on Thursday) for CONFIDENTIAL counseling. These former business men and women lend their expertise and knowledge to assist clients with problems and issues concerning starting, managing and growing a small business. ALL SERVICES AND RESOURCES ARE PROVIDED TO YOU AT NO COST! Call (816) 374-6675 to make appointments for counseling or to use the computer resources. Appointments are not necessary to use the reference library.

Other Counseling Opportunities

SCORE Chapter 19 also provides on-site counseling at the One Stop Capital Shop located in Kansas City, Kansas. Call (913) 371-6007 for additional information on the One Stop Capital Shop.

Arrangements can be made to meet with SCORE counselors at locations other than the BIC and One Stop Capital Shop. These arrangements are made between the client and the individual counselor.
