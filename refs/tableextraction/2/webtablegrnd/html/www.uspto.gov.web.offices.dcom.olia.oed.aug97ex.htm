AUGUST 1997 EXAM RESULTS

The results of the August 1997 Registration Examination were mailed to all candidates on January 22, 1998 . If you took the examination and have not received your results by January 31 , please contact Ms. Patricia Jordan at the Office of Enrollment and Discipline, (703) 306-4097.

Included in the packages sent to candidates who did not pass both parts of the examination was 1) a copy of the individual’s answer sheet for each failed section; 2) instructions for requesting a regrade of the examination; and 3) an application package for the August 1998 Registration Examination. If you did not pass a section of the examination and a copy of your paper and/or instructions for requesting a regrade were not included with your notification, please contact Ms. Shirley Brown at (703) 306-4097. Regrades must be requested in writing by March 22, 1998, and must contain the appropriate fee to be considered by the Office.

The passing rate for the 1997 examination was 58%. The passing rate for the morning section was 73%, and for the afternoon section the passing rate was 56%. There were 3,162 individuals who took the examination.
