Membership Categories



PRIVATE SECTOR MEMBER



Membership is established through payment of annual subscription to CERF, based on a company's total annual gross billings.

PUBLIC SECTOR ASSOCIATE MEMBER

Membership is established through payment of the current minimum CAB subscription rate. The public sector is represented by government-sponsored public entities, state, municipal, and local government agencies. U.S. federal agencies are not eligible for this membership category.



Annual investment
$1,000 All Levels

FEDERAL GOVERNMENT MEMBERSHIP



Federal Government agency membership is by invitation only. There is no corresponding subscription rate; rather, it is anticipated that federal government representatives will be amenable to considering joint sponsorship of research projects applicable to the interest and needs of their respective government organizations.
