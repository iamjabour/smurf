Fees, Notes and Tuition

Annual Enrollment Fee

All students are charged an annual enrollment fee annually upon acceptance or at annual re-enrollment. This fee covers the administrative cost of application or re-enrollment along with the educational materials purchased by the school. An annual enrollment fee for students in grades Kindergarten through fifth grades is $100 and $200 for sixth through eighth grades. This fee is payable at acceptance and/or re-enrollment, refundable through June 15th less a $25 change fee. Applications for enrollment after June 15th will only be accepted with the payment attached.

Tuition Discounts

In an effort to make a Grace Christian School education as affordable as possible, four discounts are available to qualifying parents.

  • Prepayment -- A parent who pays the total annual tuition due in full by August 1 receives a 3.0% tuition discount.

  • Grace Chapel Members -- GCS is a ministry of Grace Chapel . As the sponsoring church, Grace Chapel invests over $100,000 annually in direct payments to offset school expenses. The church offers a discount to those who made the commitment of membership in Grace Chapel. All members must meet the following criteria:

    • be consistent in attendance at church services and activities
    • remain on the active Grace Chapel membership roll

    The annual discount is $500 for grades one through eight, and $250 for Kindergarten. To initially qualify, you must present a Certificate of Membership at the school office. To annually qualify, complete and return the Grace Chapel Member Discount Request Card .

  • Ministe r -- Full time, salaried ministers qualify for a 15% tuition discount. Inquire at the school office for details.

  • Multiple Children -- The tuition schedule available at the school office is already computed with the discount. The oldest enrolled child is charged full price. Discounts for additional enrolled first through eighth grade students are $400 for the second child, $800 for the third child and $1000 for the fourth child or more. Discounts for enrolled Kindergarten students are $200 for the second student, $400 for the third child and $500 for the fourth child or more.

Fiscal Responsibility

  • Enrollment Fee -- Due at re-enrollment or at the time application is made.
  • Tuition Fees -- Annual tuition can be prepaid, due by August 1 (see Prepayment Discount above) or parents may pay tuition in eleven (11) equal payments, due on the first of each month, August 1, 2000 through June 1, 2001. Tuition is paid in advance for the following month of school.
  • Program Fee (see table above ) -- These fees are billed along with the tuition fees and are due on the first of the following month with tuition payment. Program fees are not included in the published tuition schedule.
  • Change Fee -- A $25 change fee will be assessed when a parent changes his/her payment agreement plan, or changes enrollment status.
  • Fiscal Responsibility Policy -- If an account is in arrears $50 or more as of the 8th of the month following billing that 30 days earlier, a late fee of $10 will be assessed at the end of business that day or the first business day following the 8th. Late fees will be applied each month that the account balance is in arrears $50 or more.



    The school will communicate with the responsible party sixty days after statement date if the account balance continues to exceed $50. An account that still exceeds $50 in arrears ninety days after statement date is cause for disenrollment, unless a payment contract has already been put into place. If the responsible party has a balance due on July 1st, the family will not be permitted to re-enroll in the fall. No student records or transcripts will be sent on behalf of students until the account is satisfied.



    Statements are mailed on the 10th of each month or the first business day following if the the 10th falls on a weekend or holiday. Payments are always due in full on the 1st of each month.

  • Refunding of Tuition/Fees -- The annual enrollment fee is refundable up through June 15 each year less a $25 change fee. Enrollment fees for applications made after June 15th are nonrefundable. Parents are responsible for any tuition and fees for services or goods already received and once fees are billed. A request for disenrollment must be made in writing to the school prior to the next month's billing (i.e., prior to the 10th of the month) in order to avoid further financial responsibility.
