Digimarc Corporation to Report Fourth Quarter Financial Results Wednesday, February 7, 2001



Q4 Earnings Conference Call

February 7, 2 p.m. (Pacific)

TUALATIN, Ore., January 30, 2001 - Digimarc Corporation (NASDAQ: DMRC), the world leader in digital watermark technology and applications, plans to report financial results for the Fourth Quarter ending December 31, 2000, on Wednesday, February 7, 2001 at 1:15 p.m. PST/4:15 p.m. EST after the close of market.

Following the earnings release, Digimarc will conduct the Fourth Quarter 2000 earnings conference call beginning at 2:00 p.m. PST/5:00 p.m. EST. Bruce Davis, Digimarc president and CEO and E.K. Ranjit, Digimarc CFO will host the call.

The call will be available live by Webcast and for replay in the following ways:

  • The live Webcast and audio archive will be accessible on the Digimarc Website at: www.digimarc.com or via www.streetfusion.com for one week following the live call.
  • A telephone replay will also be available for 24 HOURS FOLLOWING THE LIVE CALL by dialing (800) 633 8284 domestic or (858) 812 6440 internationally and entering reservation number 17273289.

Please access the Website at least fifteen minutes prior to the start of the call to download and install any necessary audio software.

About Digimarc

Digimarc Corp., based in Tualatin, Ore., (NASDAQ: DMRC) is the world leader in digital watermark technology and applications. Digimarc is creating an inherent, persistent digital identity for all media content through imperceptible digital code that can be embedded in any analog or digital material. The company has established three business units to focus on diverse media applications. Digimarc Media Commerce watermarking solutions enable protection and tracking, and also facilitate e-commerce of digital images, audio, and video. Digimarc Secure Documents provides anti-counterfeiting solutions to deter unauthorized PC-based reproduction of valuable documents such as financial, identification and product packaging. Digimarc MediaBridge - a fundamentally new way for consumers to access the Internet - allows printed materials to become direct portals to relevant destinations on the Internet. Digimarc's vision is to become a standard feature of all media content.
