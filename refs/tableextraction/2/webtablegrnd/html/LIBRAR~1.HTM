    Site Desciption

    Everything you need to know about smart stock investing can be found at this excellent, informative, and well- organized site. InvestSmart covers investment basics, and provides market simulations, where students can get hands-on experience investing $100,000 of fantasy money. Also included are investment lessons, a glossary of financial terms, and real-life examples of how three students got started in investing. Languages: English.
