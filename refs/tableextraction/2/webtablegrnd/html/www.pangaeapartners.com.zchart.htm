NEWS:

Zambia Sugar. AGM for financial year ended 31 March 2001 will be held on or about 27 June 2001. Venue to be announced. Record date to receive the final dividend is 7 th May 2001. Mandatory offer in place from 6 April to 7 May 2001.

Weekly Summary. The LuSE All Share Index remained constant for four days before it rose by 1.73% to 303.81 on Friday. This was due to a rise in the Zambia Sugar share price.
