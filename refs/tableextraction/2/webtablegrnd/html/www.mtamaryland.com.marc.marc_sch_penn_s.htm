b - Connecting bus service with train at BWI station. Bus will arrive BWI at 10:52 am southbound. Bus will leave BWI at 1:05 pm northbound. Bus does not operate on holidays. For accessible lift equipped bus service call 410-859-7420 before 1 pm on the business day prior to travel. Will not carry passengers locally between Martin Airport and Perryville.

r - Train stops at this station only to receive passengers.

S - ONLY trains designated with an S at the top of the column will operate on Martin Luther King, President?s, Columbus, Veterans Day and the Friday after Thanksgiving. In addition on days when snowfall exceeds 8 inches, MARC will operate this special schedule. On these days only, train 511 will stop at Odenton at 7:35am, Bowie State at 7:42am, Seabrook at 7:49am and New Carrollton at 7:53am; Train 421 will stop at West Baltimore at 11:00am and Halethorpe at 11:05am; Train 526 will stop at New Carrollton at 5:29pm and Odenton at 5:37pm. There is no MARC train service on New Year?s, Memorial, Independence (observed July 5), Labor, Thanksgiving and Christmas days. For Amtrak service on weekends and holidays call 1-800-USA-Rail.

z - MARC weekly and monthly tickets are accepted without penalty or "step-up" charge TO ABERDEEN ONLY. MARC tickets not honored between Washington and Baltimore.
