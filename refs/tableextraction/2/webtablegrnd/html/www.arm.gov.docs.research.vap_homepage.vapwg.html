ARM Value-Added Procedures Working Group



Purpose This working group consists of members of the infrastructure that represent all of the geophysical areas of ARM, along with representatives from the site scientists and instrument mentors. The primary goal of this group is to advance the science by the identification, prioritization, and development of value-added procedures (VAPs). To accomplish this goal, a conference call is held monthly, where all current and prospective new VAPs are reviewed and discussed. This call helps to foster communication between the separate geophysical areas to eliminate redundant tasks, identify potential collaborations, and fill unmet measurement needs.
