OTG Software Announces Annual Results for 2000 and Fourth Quarter on a Conference Call on January 25, 2001

BETHESDA, Md., January 17, 2001 – OTG Software, Inc. (NASDAQ: OTGS ), a leading software developer of online storage, data access and email management solutions, today announced that its fourth quarter and year ended December 31, 2000 financial results would be released after the Nasdaq market close on Thursday, January 25, 2001.

Richard Kay, Chief Executive Officer and Chairman, and Ronald Kaiser, Chief Financial Officer, will host the quarterly conference call for investors at 5:00PM EST that day.

The call will be broadcast live over the Internet and will be recorded and available for replay after the call. This Web cast can be replayed, on OTG’s Web site, www.otg.com .

About OTG Software

Headquartered in Bethesda, Maryland, OTG Software is a provider of online storage management and access solutions, including a dynamic email management solution. OTG’s software enables enterprises to store, track and retrieve data over a variety of network architectures, including the Web and storage area networks. Through its application-centric product suite, OTG offers a Web and e-commerce solution that provides robust functionality across many industries. OTG empowers storage by combining storage with access and email management. OTG’s products support over 750 storage devices, are easy to install and use, and can manage storage systems ranging in size from a single storage device to an enterprise-wide network storage system. OTG Software’s products are used by over 7,600 organizations worldwide and are available through a network of over 300 VARs, distributors, and OEMs.
