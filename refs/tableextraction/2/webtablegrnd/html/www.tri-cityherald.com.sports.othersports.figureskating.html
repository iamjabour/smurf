Kennewick's Hatch 11th at junior nationals

From Herald news services

Westminster, Colo - Brianna Hatch, 11, of Kennewick, finished 11th in her qualifying group at the 2001 USA Junior National Figure Skating Championships that concluded Sunday.

Hatch, a silver medalist for the Pacific Northwest Region, was skating in her debut at national competition.

Hatch, who has been skating for three years, competed with 36 other top skaters from across the countty for the national juvenile ladies title.

Hatch's selection for the US Junior National Team marks the first time the Tri-Cities Figure Skating Club has been represented in the ladies event.
