Fares on Metro Mariners Special Service

  • $2.50 cash only fare per person each way on Routes 870 - 875.
  • Passes and transfers are not accepted on Routes 870 - 875.
  • Children age 5 and under ride free with a fare paying adult.
  • On Sundays , Seniors, aged 65 and older, ride for $1.00 cash fare each way. (A Senior Citizen reduced fare permit may be used to show proof of age, however, no passes or transfers are accepted for fare on Mariners Special Service.) At all other times, Seniors pay $2.50 each way.
  • Route 879 serving downtown Seattle is FREE.

Weekend Games

Special bus service is available to and from weekend games beginning two hours and 45 minutes before the game. It runs about every half hour until 45 minutes before game time. For example, for a 1:05 p.m. start time, buses leave the park and ride lots listed below at about 10:20 a.m., 10:50 a.m., 11:20 a.m., 11:50 a.m. and 12:20 p.m.

Post-game, buses leave from their designated Safeco Field stops, shown on the map starting ten minutes after the end of the game.

  • Metro Route 870 serving the Northgate Park & Ride Lot and the Northgate Transit Center only

    • Serves the Northgate P&R on 5th Ave. NE, to and from the games on weekends. Route 870 does not enter the Northgate P&R lot. No other stops on 5th Avenue.
    • Serves the Northgate Transit Center at Bay 5 to the games and at Bay 2 from the games on weekends.
    • Serves the northbound ramp on 7th Ave. NE at NE 45th St. after the game.
    • Weekend service to the games does NOT serve the southbound stop on the NE 45th St. ramp to I-5; ride regular Metro service to games from all University District and Wallingford locations.
    • Post-game at Safeco Field, board Route 870 eastbound on S Royal Brougham Way just east of Occidental Ave. S.
  • Metro Route 871 serving the South Kirkland Park and Ride Lot
    • No stops in either direction between Safeco Field and South Kirkland.
    • Post-game at Safeco Field, board Route 871 northbound on 1st Ave. S just north of S Atlantic St.
  • Metro Route 872 serving the Eastgate and South Bellevue Park and Ride lots
    • Post-game outbound trips only on Route 872 also serve the North Mercer Island P&R.
    • For inbound service from Mercer Island at any time, ride Metro Route 215 or Sound Transit Express Route 550
    • Post-game at Safeco Field, board Route 872 northbound on 1st Ave. S just south of S Royal Brougham Way.
  • Metro Route 874 serving the Federal Way and Star Lake Park & Ride lots and the Kent-Des Moines Freeway stop

    • Serves the Federal Way P&R/Transit Center at Bay 2 to the games and Bay 1 from the games on weekends.
    • Stops inside the Star Lake P&R going to the games on weekends.
    • Serves the Southbound Star Lake Freeway Station on the east side of the P&R returning from the games.
    • Post-game at Safeco field board Route 874 southbound on 1st Ave. S just north of S Atlantic St.
  • Metro Route 875 serving the Kent, South Renton and Tukwila Park and Ride lots
    • To and from the games, no stops except at the Kent, South Renton and Tukwila Park and Ride lots.
    • Post-game at Safeco Field, board Route 875 southbound on 1st Ave. S, mid-block between S Atlantic and S Massachusetts Streets.
  • Metro Route 879 provides FREE post-game Shuttle service to Downtown Seattle
    • Prior to the end of the game, board Route 879 northbound on 1st Ave. S just south of Atlantic St. When the game ends, Route 879 is moved north.
    • After the game ends, board Route 879 northbound on 1st Ave. S just north of S Royal Brougham Way.
    • Until further notice, Route 879 travels to Union St on 1st Ave. S for Downtown connections to regularly scheduled bus service.

  • Metro's Waterfront Streetcar (Route 99) operates regular schedules (no extra trips on game days) and charges regular fares at all times

  • Ride Sound Transit Express Route 550 ( regular ST fare ) or Metro Route 215 ( regular Metro fare ) from the North Mercer Island Park & Ride to games; return on Metro Route 872 ($2.50 cash fare per person).

Weekday Games

Regular service to the game. For Mariners games during the week, ride regularly scheduled service to the games. See the Park & Ride pages to choose a park and ride lot near you for regular service. See the map that shows regular Metro bus routes that serve Safeco Field . Regular Metro fares are in effect on all regularly scheduled service and the Waterfront Streetcar.

Special service is provided only from designated Safeco Field stops shown on the map starting ten minutes after each game. Buses depart as they fill and cannot be held. Fans are encouraged to proceed directly to boarding points for service.

  • Metro Route 870 to NE 45th St., the Northgate Transit Center and the Northgate Park & Ride Lot

    • Post-game at Safeco Field, board Route 870 eastbound on S Royal Brougham Way just east of Occidental Ave. S.
    • Stops northbound on 7th Ave. NE at NE 45th St.
    • Serves the Northgate Transit Center, Bay 2.
    • Serves the Northgate P&R, northbound on 5th Ave. NE.
  • Metro Route 871 to the South Kirkland Park and Ride Lot
    • Post-game at Safeco Field, board Route 871 northbound on 1st Ave. S just north of S Atlantic St.
    • No stops between Safeco Field and South Kirkland.
  • Metro Route 872 to the North Mercer Island, South Bellevue and Eastgate Park and Ride lots
    • Post-game at Safeco Field, board Route 872 northbound on 1st Ave. S just south of S Royal Brougham Way.
    • For service to the game from Mercer Island at any time, ride Metro Route 215 or Sound Transit Express Route 550.
  • Metro Route 874 to the Kent Des Moines and Star Lake Freeway stations and the Federal Way Park and Ride Lot
    • Post-game at Safeco Field, board Route 874 southbound on 1st Ave. S just north of S Atlantic St.
  • Metro Route 875 serving the Tukwila, South Renton and Kent Park and Ride lots
    • Post-game at Safeco Field, board Route 875 southbound on 1st Ave. S, mid-block between S Atlantic and S Massachusetts Streets.
  • Metro Route 879 provides FREE post-game Shuttle service to Downtown Seattle
    • Prior to the end of the game, board Route 879 northbound on 1st Ave. S just south of Atlantic St. When the game ends, Route 879 is moved north.
    • After the game ends, board Route 879 northbound on 1st Ave. S just north of S Royal Brougham Way.
    • Until further notice, Route 879 travels to Union St on 1st Ave. S for Downtown connections to regularly scheduled bus service.
  • Metro's Waterfront Streetcar (Route 99) operates regular schedules (no extra trips on game days) and charges regular fares at all times
  • Ride Sound Transit Express Route 550 ( regular ST fare ) or Metro Route 215 ( regular Metro fare ) from the North Mercer Island Park & Ride to games; return on Metro Route 872 ($2.50 cash fare per person)

There is no special service for the following weekday games * . These games are during peak commuter hours when buses operate plenty of regular service:

  • Thursday, June 28th - 3:35 p.m. scheduled start
  • Tuesday, July 17th - 1:05 p.m. scheduled start
  • Thursday, August 23rd - 3:35 p.m. scheduled start
  • Thursday, September 20th - 3:35 p.m. scheduled start
* On the above days you are advised to expect severe traffic delays in the Downtown, Safeco Field and "SODO" areas. Using Transit and sharing rides will help to alleviate the congestion. Please plan accordingly.

The Metro Tunnel closes at 7:00 p.m. on weekdays and 6:00 p.m. on Saturdays. The Tunnel is closed on Sundays and holidays. If you miss the last Mariners Special from Safeco Field and your alternate route is a Tunnel route and the Tunnel is closed, board your bus on a surface street. Tunnel routes operate in both directions along Third Avenue between Yesler Way and Olive Way.

Call Metro Rider Information at (206) 553-3000, TTY users call (206) 684-1739, to find the best route to the game.

Sounder Rail Special Home Run Service

Sound Transit will operate Sounder train Home Run Service from the Tacoma Dome Station to the King Street Station for Sunday games with 1:35 p.m. start times and the two All Star events. Sounder Home Run Service will operate on the following days:

  • June 3, 10 & 24
  • July 7, 8, 15 & 29
  • August 12 & 26
  • September 9, 16 & 30
For the games listed above, the Sounder train leaves the Tacoma Dome Station at 11:00 AM. Parking is free at Sounder Commuter Rail stations (no parking at King Street Station). Home Run Service does not stop at the Tacoma Amtrak Station. The schedule is as follows:
Tacoma Dome Station 11:00 a.m.
Puyallup 11:13 a.m.
Sumner 11:18 a.m.
Auburn 11:27 a.m.
Kent 11:34 a.m.
Tukwila 11:41 a.m.
Arrive Seattle - King Street Station, approximately Noon

The special Sounder train leaves from Seattle's King Street Station for the return trip at 5:35 p.m. sharp, even if the game goes into extra innings.

Depending on where you board, round trip fares on Home Run Service are $8.00 or $6.00 for adults, $6 or $4.50 for Youths, aged 5-18 and $4 or $3.00 for Seniors and riders with disabilities. Children age 4 and under ride free. Tickets must be purchased in advance. Puget Passes and Sounder Scratch tickets are not valid for Home Run Service trips, and your ticket cannot be used as a transfer to other Transit service. For complete information, access the Sounder web site , and click on 'Home Run Service'.
