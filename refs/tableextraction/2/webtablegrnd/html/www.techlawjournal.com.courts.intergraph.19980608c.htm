STATEMENT OF SUBJECT MATTER

AND APPELLATE JURISDICTION

The underlying action in the district court is a patent infringement suit filed under 28 U.S.C. § 1338 by plaintiff-appellee Intergraph Corporation against defendant-appellant Intel Corporation. The District Court issued a preliminary injunction order on April 10, 1998. Intel Corporation filed a notice of appeal from that order on April 16, 1998. Accordingly, this Court has jurisdiction over Intel's appeal from the district court's grant of preliminary injunctive relief pursuant to 28 U.S.C. §§ 1292(a)(1), 1292(c)(1), and 1295(a)(1) (1998).
