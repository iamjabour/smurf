Market Makers
The CME lists Heating Degree Day (HDD) and Cooling Degree Day (CDD) futures and options on futures on ten cities in the United States. This CME Weather Derivative Resource Center provides informational links to an online version of the CME weather derivative brochure, analytical papers on the weather derivative market, contract specifications, a guide to vendor symbols as well as links to temperature data on the ten cities on which contracts are listed.
