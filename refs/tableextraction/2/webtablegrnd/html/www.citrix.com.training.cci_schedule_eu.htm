New Train the Trainer Format

Beginning in Q3 2001, the Train the Trainer (TTT) format in EMEA will be four days long, and have a much stronger emphasis on trainer preparation and teach-back. Classroom participation is paramount. During the last three days of the course, candidates will be expected to deliver at least one 15 minute presentation per day on topics found in the CTX-1220 course. The course will also cover delivery techniques as well as detailing many topics normally beyond the scope of the courseware. On the final day, each student will be required to deliver a longer presentation (about one hour) on a topic found in course CTX-1220. Students will be apprised of their progress each day and each day's scores will be used to determine if the candidate successfully completes the course. See the attached Q&A and syllabus for details.
