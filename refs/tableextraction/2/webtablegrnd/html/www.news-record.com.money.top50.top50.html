Triad's 2000 top 50 public companies



5-14-00

The companies ranked are either based in the Triad or have significant operations here. Companies are ranked relative to each other in four categories: 2-year total return to investors 1999-1998; 1999 total revenues; revenue growth in 1999; and net income growth in 1999. Rankings in each category were added together to give each company a composite score. The lower the score, the higher the overall rank in the Top 50. Example: Lucent ranked No. 2 in 2-year return, No. 7 in revenues, No. 13 in revenue growth, and No. 5 in net income growth, giving it a composite score of 27 (2+7+13+5=27). Revenues are in thousands of dollars (add ,000 to dollar amounts).
