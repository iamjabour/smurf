The My-Cast Difference

My-Cast calculates weather forecasts eight times daily in four-mile sections across most metropolitan areas. Eleven-mile sections cover the rest of the U.S. By comparison, National Weather Forecasts update four times a day across 20-mile sections .

We offer weather forecasts precisely for your locations . Check back often for updates on how the weather will affect your plans and favorite outdoor activities.

My-Cast is available via the Web and personalized email updates. Coming soon to wireless devices!

NEW! My-Cast ® Personal Radar

With My-Cast Personal Radar, you and your registered locations are placed at the center of the map. Watch how the rain or snow will impact the locations you care about . The radar images update every 5-10 minutes , giving you a fresh look at how the storm is moving in.

Other Special Features:

Loop ? turn the loop on to track the movement of the precipitation

Zoom ? zoom in to isolate your location on the map; zoom out for a broader scope

Change locations ? Personal Radar covers all of your registered locations.
