How to enter :
  1. Play a game and try to get as high a score as possible (only do this once or twice because it's very time-consuming).
    • Please only play on randomly-generated maps or world maps. We no longer accept custom map games.
    • DO NOT CHEAT! If you use Cheat mode, your game will NOT be added even if it's 3000%.
  2. Save your game just BEFORE the game ends, either by conquest, spaceship, or retirement.
  3. Send it in! Be sure to include the following information in your email:
    • the saved game
    • your name
    • difficulty level
    • your Civ2 version
    • map type (random or world map)
    • score and percentage rating
    • directions on how to finish the game
