    J.N.R. Score Quadrangle

    Named for the 10th president of Southwestern, J.N.R. Score, this area features the F.W. Olin Building on the west and the Corbin J. Robertson Center on the east. It is the site for campus parties and receptions and is where each graduating class has its photograph taken following Commencement.
