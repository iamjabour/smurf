Panafrican News Agency (Dakar)

March 29, 2001

> Posted to the web March 29, 2001

Rabat, Morocco

Morocco will host from 3 to 7 April the fourth African table tennis cup winner's cup.

The Confederation of African Table Tennis (CATT) is confident in Morocco's ability to host the event, Tayeb M'hamdi, chairman of the Royal Moroccan Table Tennis Federation and vice- chairman of CATT, has said.

Morocco has already hosted the Arab champions' Cup, the Arab Union Cup and the Arab Cup of Nations.

The country will be represented by three clubs in the tournament - FUS of Rabat, Ittihad Zemmouri of Khemisset and Club Amitie of Mohammedia.
