Over 2,900 Social Security Administration (SSA) and Disability Determination Service (DDS) employees responded to the 2010 Vision Employee Survey . The survey was designed to record the impressions of employees to the Vision . The following is a summary of the survey results.

Note: Responses to questions number 1, "Please indicate where you work" and 3, "How did you hear about the Vision" were not recorded in these summary results.

Methodology

SSA:
  • 800 number survey conducted by Gelfond Group
  • 6,275 surveys mailed
  • 2,642 responses
  • accuracy: 95% validity +/- 2%

DDS:

  • “Mail-in” Survey
  • 500 mailed
  • 321 responses
