Introduction

Since The Accounting Library supports a diverse population of users, consultants and resellers, we have elected to divide our web into smaller components depending upon the information you might require. In addition, the table of contents itself has been formatted in three different ways.

  • Summary Level I : This chart lists only the header pages themselves.
  • Summary Level II : This chart lists each header page as well as each major subsection in the page. In some cases, we have had to split one subject area into separate pages. This is indicated by the indents in the Table of Contents and/or by a notation such as "(Page 2)".
  • Detailed : The Detailed Table of Contents adds descriptions so that you can understand better exactly what subjects are addressed.
