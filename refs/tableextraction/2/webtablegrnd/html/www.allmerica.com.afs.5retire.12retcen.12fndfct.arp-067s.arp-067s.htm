The Stable Value Fund invests in high-quality corporate and U.S. government and agency fixed income securities. It seeks to provide a stable rate of return that is responsive to changes in interest rates while also protecting against loss of principal.

Commentary

Funds invested in the Stable Value Fund are guaranteed with respect to both principal and a floor rate of return. A new interest rate will be determined at the beginning of each calendar quarter based on the expected book value yield to maturity of the underlying assets of the Fund. The book value guarantee of the Fund results in a smoothing of the total return volatility normally associated with a bond fund.

Allmerica Asset Management will identify inefficiencies in the fixed income market, integrate economic and fundamental security research and actively manage duration, maturity, sectors and individual securities to enhance return potential.
