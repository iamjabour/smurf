RSA Security Announces Record Q3 Results

BEDFORD, Mass., October 12, 2000 ? RSA Security Inc. (Nasdaq: RSAS) today reported financial results for the third quarter ended September 30, 2000. Revenue for the third quarter of 2000 increased 29% to $72.0 million, from $55.7 million for the third quarter of 1999. Reported net income for the third quarter of 2000, including the operations of RSA Capital, was $40.0 million ($0.94 per share on a diluted basis), compared to $58.6 million ($1.42 per share on a diluted basis) for the third quarter of 1999. Reported net income for the third quarter of 2000 includes a pre-tax gain of $51.0 million from the sale of shares of the company's holdings in public companies. Earnings for the quarter from the company's core operating business increased 38% to $10.2 million ($0.24 per share on a diluted basis) from $7.4 million ($0.18 per share on a diluted basis) for the same period in 1999.

Revenue for the nine months ended September 30, 2000, was $202.1 million, a 29% increase over the same period last year. Net income from the company's core operating business increased 53% to $28.1 million, or $0.65 per diluted share, compared to $18.4 million, or $0.44 per diluted share, in the first nine months of 1999. Reported net income for the first nine months of 2000 was $101.8 million, compared to $142.6 million for the same period last year. Reported net income for the first nine months of 2000 includes pre-tax gains of $123.0 million from the sale of shares of the company's holdings in public companies.

"We are very pleased with the company's performance this quarter, as we once again beat expectations and exceeded internal goals for every product line," said Art Coviello, CEO and president of RSA Security. "These results are further proof that what RSA Security does better than any other company is enable our customers to ensure the authenticity of people, devices and transactions - all of which are critical elements of every secure e-business environment."

Highlights

During the third quarter, revenue for the company's public key infrastructure (PKI) solutions increased 34% sequentially, as RSA Security signed contracts with several leading organizations, including:

  • Ford Motor Company
  • Parametric Technologies
  • Global Network Privacy
  • ABB Germany

The company's RSA BSAFE ® encryption products set a new record for revenues and added several significant new licensees for wireless applications, including:

  • Matsushita
  • Broadvision
  • National Semiconductor

RSA Security also introduced the next version of its RSA BSAFE Crypto products, which incorporate Compaq's MultiPrime technology licensed from Compaq that is designed to increase the performance of RSA calculations by as much as 500%.

During the quarter, the company sold its seven millionth RSA SecurID ® authenticator and delivered a total of more than 661,000 RSA SecurID authenticators. Demand for RSA SecurID authentication solutions continues to increase based on strong growth in the virtual private network (VPN) and online application markets.

On October 4, RSA Security announced that its Board of Directors has authorized the company to repurchase up to an additional 4 million shares of its common stock over the next 12 months.

About RSA Security Inc.

RSA Security Inc., The Most Trusted Name in e-Security?, helps organizations build secure, trusted foundations for e-business through its RSA SecurID ® two-factor authentication, RSA BSAFE ® encryption and RSA Keon ® digital certificate management systems. With more than a half billion RSA BSAFE-enabled applications in use worldwide, more than seven million RSA SecurID users and almost 20 years of industry experience, RSA Security has the proven leadership and innovative technology to address the changing security needs of e-business and bring trust to the new, online economy. RSA Security can be reached at www.rsasecurity.com .
