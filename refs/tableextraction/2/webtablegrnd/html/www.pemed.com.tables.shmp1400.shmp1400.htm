Shampaigne 1400 Surgical Tables - $8,500 each

List: $36,700. MedCon: VG. Shampaigne model 1400 surgical table with radio-op top. Radioluscent top on full surgical table. This table may be used for most surgical procedures. It is both portable x-ray and C-arm compatible. New in May, 1985.

Table motions:

  • longitudinal slide action (lengthwise), both forward and backward deflections
  • lateral tilt, both left and right
  • height adjust, up and down
  • Trendelenberg and reverse Trendelenburg tilting
  • kidney support, up and down
  • leg up / down
  • back up / down

This is how this table fits a C-arm (patient and C-arm not included)

Tables features:

  • Small control panel, accessible from side and end of table
  • roll-around castors with positive floor locking action once table is in position in OR
  • All stainless steel construction
  • table top moves with 7 degrees of freedom
  • Table geometry and design allows full access to patient with a C-arm
  • with adjustable angle table extension that may be put in either the foot or head of table
  • with standard surgical table side channels for accepting surgical table accessories such as arm boards, kidney supports, stirrups, etc. (not included with table)

4 matching units in stock. And, we have a copy of the service manual.
