VISIT OF THE SECRETARY-GENERAL TO WASHINGTON, D.C.

24 May 2001

( Subject to Change)

Thursday, 24th

Depart New York

Meeting with Rep. Richard Gephardt (Democratic Minority Leader) and other Democratic Leaders

Meeting with Chairman of the House International Relations Committee, Henry Hyde, and members of the Committee, including ranking minority member Tom Lantos. Secretary-General to speak and take questions

Private lunch

Meeting with Washington Post Editorial Board

Meeting with members of the Senate Foreign Relations Committee, including Chairman Jesse Helms and ranking Democratic member Joseph Biden

Meeting with Senate Democratic Leader Tom Daschle, and members of the Senate Appropriations Committee

Return to New York

All other appointments are internal.
