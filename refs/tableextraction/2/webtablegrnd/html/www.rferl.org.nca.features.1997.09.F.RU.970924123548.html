World: UN Data On Direct Foreign Investment--A Table

By Robert Lyle

Hong Kong, 24 September 1997 (RFE/RL) - The United Nations Conference on Trade and Development (UNCTAD) earlier this week issued its World Investment Report detailing direct foreign investment flows into all countries in the world.

This is a list of data for RFE/RL target areas. The table presents figures in millions of dollars by region, then by country.
