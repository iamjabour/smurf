2001 Excursion Schedule

The Wisconsin & Southern Railroad Co. is planning to operate the following excursions. All trips will run regardless of the weather and feature the Wisconsin & Southern’s Executive E-9 locomotives and streamlined train coaches with heating and air conditioning, restrooms, reclining seats, and leg rests. Some trips will have First Class equipment on the train, including Dome and Compartment service, and our baggage car with open, gated doors. Our Company Store may also be available on some trips.

Ticket Reservations: Contact the sponsor of the trip directly for ticket prices, availability, reservations or more information. For trips sponsored by the Wisconsin & Southern , tickets may be purchased using your credit card (MasterCard or Visa) by calling either (608) 241-9767 or (414) 438-8820 between 8 AM and 4 PM Monday through Friday. Please add $4.00 shipping and handling per order if tickets are to be mailed. There may be on-line order forms found under the description for each trip.

The Wisconsin & Southern Railroad reserves the right to alter schedules, substitute equipment or cancel trips with no notice.

Safety Notice

The Wisconsin & Southern is an active railroad and all tracks are in use by passenger and freight trains on a daily basis. Use extreme caution around any tracks, watch in all directions for train movements and do not climb on any equipment or walk on tracks. In addition, footing may be poor in many areas. Please watch your step at all times.
