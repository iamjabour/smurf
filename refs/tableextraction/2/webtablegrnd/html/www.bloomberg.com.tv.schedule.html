Saturday Mornings

6:00AM to 6:30AM BLOOMBERG SMALL BUSINESS is a special half-hour version of Bloomberg Television. Each week, Jeff Weiser covers topic of interest to small businesses. The BLOOMBERG TELEVISION Data Screen provides useful information on the topic as well. Special guests are featured every week.
6:30AM to 7:00AM BLOOMBERG PERSONAL® FINANCE is another special half-hour version of Bloomberg Television. Its focus is personal finance. Saving for your child's college education, your retirement or a well-earned vacation? Don't miss this informative and insightful show. Jeff Weiser is your host.
Other Weekend Programming
All weekend long, BLOOMBERG TELEVISION provides a comprehensive digest of the week's business news and a look ahead to Monday and beyond. We also provide complete updates of general news. While the markets might be closed, the BLOOMBERG TELEVISION Data Screen never stops delivering a constant stream of information for you. Finally, remember that come Sunday night, it is already Monday morning in Tokyo. Our financial market reporters in the Bloomberg Tokyo bureau are on the air tracking the Asian markets.
