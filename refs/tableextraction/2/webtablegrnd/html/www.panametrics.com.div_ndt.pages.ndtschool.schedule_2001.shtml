IN-PLANT TRAINING COURSES
Panametrics offers custom tailored, on-site training courses. In-plant courses are arranged to meet your exact requirements and can cover one or more topics Benefits of in-plant courses:
  • Cost Effective
  • Flexible Scheduling
  • Course is held at a location of your choice
  • Custom tailored to your needs

In-plant training is offered on the following subjects:

  • Level I
  • Level II
  • Advanced Detection & Sizing
  • Thickness Gauging

Schools can cover specialized applications.

Panametrics provides the following for each class:

  • A Qualified Instructor
  • Textbooks & Handouts
  • Flaw Detectors, Transducers and Various Test Samples
  • Course Completion Certificates

The Client Provides:

  • Classroom/Laboratory Facility
