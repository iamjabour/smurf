Recent Research Results (RRR) is prepared by HUD USER, the information service sponsored by HUD's Office of Policy Development and Research (PD&R), to provide ready access to research information.

RRR contains short summaries of reports recently published under the auspices of PD&R. In addition to disseminating Recent Research Results , HUD USER produces a bibliographic database and provides complete reference and document distribution services, including resource guides, computer packages, blueprints, and audiovisual materials.
