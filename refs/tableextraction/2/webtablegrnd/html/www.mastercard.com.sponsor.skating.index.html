World Figure Skating is growing in popularity by leaps, bounds, and figure eights. And no wonder! Today's gifted young skaters combine athletic talent and finely-honed skills with a rare blend of grace, style and wit.





MasterCard recently signed world-famous skater and 1997 World Champion Elvis Stojko as its official spokesperson. Elvis is the first man to ever perform a quadruple jump combination in competition (quad toe loop-double toe loop). Other awards include:
  • Silver Medal: 1994 Olympic Winter Games
  • Gold Medal: 1994 World Championships
  • Gold Medal: 1995 World Championships
  • Gold Medal: 1996 and 1997 Canadian Figure Skating Championships
MasterCard is proud and honored to have Elvis as its official World Figure Skating spokesperson. We look forward to great things for this gifted young man.



As Sponsor and Official Card of World Figure Skating, MasterCard is pleased to continue its support and commitment to the sport of figure skating and the future of bright young skaters around the world. The best is yet to come.
