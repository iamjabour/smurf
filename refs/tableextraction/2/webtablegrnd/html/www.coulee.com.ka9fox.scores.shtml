How to get your score listed here

Michael Dinkelman, ( mwdink@eskimo.com ) has volunteered to collect scores for a couple of weeks after the contest. You can submit your score to him Via the NEW ON-LINE SCORE SUBMISSION FORM http://www.hornucopia.com/3830score/ , where your submission will be visible to everyone that checks the 3830 Web Page on Contesting.com Remember, don't send your logs to Mike or the 3830, only your total QSOs, multipliers, etc., and final bottom-line score.
