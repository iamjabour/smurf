CONFERENCE OVERVIEW

This page contains an overview of the conference schedule and social events for the 1996 ACR conference. You may also download the entire program file in either MS Word for Windows format (acrprog.doc, 132K ) , WordPerfect format (acrprog.wpd, 163K ), or Rich Text Format (acrprog.rtf, 137K ). You may need to set your browser options to define these extensions appropriately.

Each session is hyper-linked to more details on that session including paper abstracts and presenters. Please click on any of the highlighted sessions to get the detailed information. Also, check the list of Roundtable Sessions to be held during the conference.

    Newcomers Breakfast (all first time ACR attendees are welcome!)

    Location: Ventana Room
  • 11:55 a.m. - 1:15 p.m.

    Luncheon (no speakers)

    Location: Kiva Ballroom
  • 5:00 p.m. - 9:00 p.m.

    Dining in the Desert Trip

    (Bus trip to dine in the desert and experience the cultural and natural beauty of the Southwest. Activities include hiking into the National Forest, visiting ancient Indian petrogliphs, enjoying an authentic Mexican meal (watch tortillas being made by hand), star-gazing with UA astronomer, learning the craft of raising Sonoran desert plants, making Southwest jewelry, and making authentic Southwestern salas (and more). Salsas and jewelry for sale!)

    Location: Two busses - One leaves from the lobby at 5:00. The other leaves from the lobby at 5:45. Take the 5:00 bus if you want to go hiking
