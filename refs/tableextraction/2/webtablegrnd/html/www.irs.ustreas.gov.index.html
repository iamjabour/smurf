Connecticut Store Manager Takes Advantage of IRS e-file Benefits

Moosup, CT. Michael Mullen, store manager for the Moosup Meat Company, filed for an extension of time to file his taxes on April 16th. As it turns out, Michael is due a large refund and plans to take his wife and two children to Washington, DC, to go sightseeing.

Last year Michael and his wife, Joanna, e-filed and plan to do so again this year for FREE using an IRS e-file Partner . He has been telling his family and friends, " E-filing is the fastest most accurate way to file your taxes." You can e-file even after the tax due date has passed (tax payment is still required by April 16th) up until October 15th. Michael is elated with the benefits of e-filing :

  • It's more accurate than mailing a paper return (less than a 1% error rate);
  • Offers an acknowledgement within 48 hours signifying that the tax return has been accepted;
  • Offers a faster and safer refund with Direct Deposit -- get your refund in as few as 10 days; and
  • Offers the convenience of filing and paying your taxes in a single step, if you owe taxes.

Michael's co-worker, Kevin Kinsley, chose to e-file his return and he paid the balance due electronically at the same time. While e-filing , he simply authorized an electronic withdrawal payment from his bank account. How convenient!

Taxpayers can also charge the taxes due by credit card and enjoy the rewards that their card issuers may offer.

IRS e-file certainly offers the most accurate, convenient, and safe way to file and pay individual income taxes!
