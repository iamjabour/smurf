Software Subscription for AIX

> Highlights
>



Subscription offering for upgrades to AIX® and related software products provides price protection and the convenience of a single, enterprise-wide administration point

Available without any minimum order requirements

Annual (1) billing with automatic renewal (2) reduces administrastrative costs

Automatic notification and online ordering of upgrades provides better service

Ability to order multiple media supply types with quantities on the same order (U.S. and Canada only) for additional flexibility.





> Description
>



Software Subscription for AIX provides RS/6000 AIX® customers with a stream lined process to keep their system's software current. The new, "no-signature" contract provides price protection and the convenience of a single, enterprise-wide administration point. All RS/6000 customers can now use the Web to sign up for notification via e-mail (3) of new AIX releases. After receiving notification, customers can also use the Web toorder (4) delivery of the new releases. This convenience, combined with automatic contract renewal, greatly simplifies software maintenance administration and helps improve expense management. This program is available with no minimum order requirements to all RS/6000 customers.

Software Subscription for AIX replaces the existing two-year Upgrade Protection program, Software Upgrade Protection Plan for AIX and UNIX AIX® operating System Based Software(5692-ADV). Existing contracts for the Upgrade Protection program(5692-ADV) will be honored but will not be renewed, extended or modified. Software Subscription is extremely flexible. All of the software products you select, including products you add throughout the year, will be billed at an annual rate allowing you to both maintain a single point of administration and predictably manage the cost of software. Periodic charge subscriptions are subject to a one-year minimum. The periodic charge will continue until the customer requests a discontinuance with a 30-day written notice.

The list of eligible products is subject to change. View the most recent exhibit .

> Notification of upgrade availablitly and ordering
>



You can register to be notified via e-mail when products have a priced upgrade.

When you are ready to take receipt of the upgrade:

  • U.S.: Fill out an online shipment request form for your upgrade or call Software Delivery and Fulfillment(SDF) at 1-800-879-2755.
  • EMEA: Order via reply letter. Reply letters are automatically shipped to customers when Version/Release upgrades are available.
  • LA/AP/Canada: Call your IBM marketing representative or IBM Business Partner.

The price is determined by the products and product options selected. Please call your IBM marketing representative or IBM Business Partner for specific price information.





> Eligible products list
>



Software upgrades are provided under the Software Subscription for AIX for the programs listed at the time of this publication. Upgrades to RS/6000 programs not eligible for Software Subscription for AIX can be ordered separately for a charge. Non-priced updates are not affected by this program.
