Site Listings



  • SERVEnet - home of Youth Service America. Site includes daily service news, online resources and volunteer opportunity postings, searchable by zip code.

  • @grass-roots.org - tells the stories of the nation's most innovative grass roots programs that are changing their communities and building individual self-reliance.
  • Action Without Borders - includes a directory of thousands of nonprofit websites, an online library for nonprofits and a database of volunteer opportunities nationwide.
  • Association of Voluntary Service Organisations (AVSO) - platform of European nonprofit organizations offering international volunteer service opportunities.
  • Bowling Alone: America's Declining Social Capital - an interview with Robert Putnam from the Journal of Democracy.
  • Community Service.org - searchable index of volunteer opportunities.
  • Hearts and Minds Network - includes links to volunteering and donating opportunities.
  • Neighborhoods Online - online resource center for people working to build strong communities throughout the United States. Includes issue guides, community development resources, and more.
  • Pitch In - educational resource promoting youth volunteerism.
  • Planet Volunteer - searchable database of volunteer opportunities.
  • Service Leader - assists agencies in recruiting volunteers and managing projects with a special emphasis on work that can be done using the Internet.
  • Volunteer for Our Children - ideas for volunteering to make a difference in the life of a child and links to volunteer opportunities. From the National Children's Coalition.
  • VolunteerMatch - matches volunteers with nonprofit organizations.
  • VolunteerWeb - provides a forum for connecting potential volunteers and organizations/agencies.
  • Web Assist Network - seeks to connect people and charities with needs to those with resources, time, and ability to help.
  • Usenet - ba.helping-hand - volunteer opportunities.
