Panafrican News Agency (Dakar)

March 22, 2001

> Posted to the web March 22, 2001

Yaounde, Cameroon

Yaounde-based Canon football club has regained the lead in the national first division championship with 17 points after the 8th round, ahead of Cotonsport and Sable which have 16 points each.

Union is last with only two points. Following are the results after the 8th round: 

Tonnerre 2 - 1 Olympic
Mount Cameroon 2 - 2 Racing
Unisport 1 - 0 Stade
Canon 1 - 0 Cintra
Dynamo1 - 1Union
Sable 2 - 0 Fovu
