Finally, we quantified energy savings in each of the three buildings. The San Jose building had a radiant barrier under the roof and the attic was ventilated. As a result, the savings at that site were minimal. In each of the buildings, we also had a kiosk equipped with a touch-screen computer that demonstrated the project.
